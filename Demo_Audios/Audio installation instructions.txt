NOTE : Adding custom audio in jq6500 
You will need 6 Audios , Audio 1,2,3,4,5 corresponds to 5 Alarm and Audio 6 is for Theft Alarm. All are in mp3 format and all combined audio size should be below or up to 2000kb / 2 mb , most important is to enter audio name as 001 - 002 - 003 … where audio 1 is 001 and audio 2 is 002 and so on. Now you can Upload it in jQ6500 Module.
